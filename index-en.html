About LIMMI

LIMMI is a natural juice from handpicked Italian lemons, grown in the sun of Sicily, Campania and Calabria. Since 1999 we have preserved tradition and guaranteed impeccable quality in every bottle.

The harvest is hand-picked at peak ripeness, and the cold-press technology preserves the maximum vitamin C and rich, vibrant flavour with no added sugar, colorants or preservatives. This makes LIMMI the market leader in Italy with a 38% share and the ideal choice for your home bar.

Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to the most common questions about LIMMI

How is LIMMI different from fresh lemon?

▼

LIMMI delivers consistent taste and convenience in every bottle. No need to wash, cut or squeeze lemons — just open and add. Perfect for your home bar.

How long does an opened bottle keep?

▼

After opening, store in the fridge at +2°C to +4°C within the best-before date. The bottle has a convenient dispenser that keeps the product fresh. The product may contain natural sediment. Shake before use.

Is LIMMI suitable for baking and cooking?

▼

Yes! LIMMI works perfectly in marinades, sauces, baking and desserts. It is a full replacement for fresh lemon juice.

Which lemons are used to make LIMMI?

▼

LIMMI is made from 100% Sicilian lemons grown on sunny Italian plantations. No concentrate, no additives.

Does it contain sugar?

▼

The classic LIMMI lemon juice contains no sugar. It is pure natural lemon juice. For sweet drinks we recommend adding syrups to taste. The sweet LIMMI version uses fructose syrup.

Where can I buy LIMMI?

▼

LIMMI is available on Wildberries, Ozon and Globus hypermarkets. Links are in the "Where to buy" section.
