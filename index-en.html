About LIMMI
LIMMI is a habit: one product that makes homemade drinks easier and more consistent.
EDUCATION-FIRST

We do not just “sell lemon juice” — we show how to use it every day

In Russia, lemon juice is often seen as an occasional substitute for fresh lemon or just a cooking ingredient. Our goal is to expand its use cases and make LIMMI a standard home bar ingredient.

  • Drink occasions: LIMMI naturally fits homemade cocktails and mixed drinks.
  • Convenience: no need to cut lemons — just open and pour.
  • Consistent result: the same taste and acidity in every bottle.
  • One bottle = dozens of servings for drinks and mixes.
